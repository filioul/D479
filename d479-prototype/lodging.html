Image credit: Sara Dubler via Unsplash

Taniti offers a wide variety of lodging options.

All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Looking for luxurious lodging?

Introducing "Azure Haven," a luxurious retreat nestled on the shores of Taniti, where opulence meets tropical splendor. Indulge in an unparalleled experience of luxury at this exclusive resort, offering elegantly appointed suites, private villas, and unparalleled amenities. Immerse yourself in the tranquil ambiance of meticulously landscaped gardens, lounge by infinity pools that merge seamlessly with the horizon, and savor exquisite culinary delights crafted by world-class chefs. Azure Haven promises a haven of relaxation, with a spa that rejuvenates the body and soul. With personalized service and breathtaking views of Yellow Leaf Bay, this sanctuary epitomizes the epitome of indulgence in the heart of Taniti. Escape to a world where every detail is meticulously curated to ensure your stay at Azure Haven is nothing short of extraordinary.
Image credit: Christian Lambert via Unsplash

Do you prefer more homely accomodations?

Experience the warmth of Taniti's hospitality with our charming family-owned hotels, cozy bed and breakfasts, and welcoming hostel. Discover the unique character of each accommodation option, where personalized service and a genuine sense of community await. Whether you choose a quaint bed and breakfast for a homely touch, a family-owned hotel for a taste of local charm, or a hostel for a vibrant, communal atmosphere, Taniti offers a range of accommodation experiences to suit every traveler. Immerse yourself in the genuine warmth of the island's families and communities, creating memories that go beyond the typical tourist experience. Choose the comfort and authenticity of these accommodations to make your stay in Taniti truly special.
