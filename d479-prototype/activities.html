Our island offers a variety of activities for you and your loved ones.

Historical Museum of Taniti

Step into the past at Taniti's Historical Museum, a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of our island's history. Immerse yourself in the tales of ancient civilizations, colonial influences, and the cultural evolution that has shaped Taniti into the vibrant paradise it is today. Join us in unraveling the threads of time, where every artifact is a treasure trove of history waiting to be explored. Located in the heart of Taniti City.
Image credit: Fakhri Labib via Unsplash

Fishing Tours

Embark on an unforgettable aquatic adventure with Taniti's Fishing Tours, where the thrill of the catch meets the serenity of the open sea. Our expert guides will lead you to the most pristine fishing spots surrounding our island, teeming with an abundance of marine life. Capture the essence of Taniti's maritime beauty as you cast your line and reel in the memories of a lifetime.
Image credit: Vincent Camacho via Unsplash

Snorkeling

Dive into the vibrant underwater world of Taniti with our exhilarating snorkeling experiences. Crystal-clear waters, teeming with a kaleidoscope of marine life, invite you to explore the captivating coral reefs that surround our island paradise. Taniti's snorkeling tours cater to all levels, from beginners to seasoned enthusiasts, ensuring an immersive and safe adventure for everyone.
Image credit: Mike L via Unsplash

Zip-lining

Taniti's zip-lining courses are carefully designed to provide a perfect blend of adrenaline-pumping excitement and awe-inspiring natural beauty. Feel the rush of wind against your face as you traverse the rainforest, surrounded by the sights and sounds of tropical flora and fauna. Our certified guides ensure a safe and thrilling journey, allowing you to unleash your inner daredevil in this pristine, untouched paradise.
Image credit: Nadiia Ganzhyi via Unsplash

Helicopter rides

Elevate your Taniti experience with our exclusive helicopter rides, offering a bird's-eye view of the island's most iconic landscapes. Soar above Taniti City, where the vibrant colors of the urban center contrast against the azure waters of Yellow Leaf Bay. Marvel at the panoramic vistas of the rainforest and volcano, revealing an emerald tapestry teeming with life.
Image credit: Karsten Winegeart via Unsplash

Taniti National Gallery

Discover the soulful expressions of Taniti's indigenous culture at the Taniti National Gallery, nestled in the heart of Taniti City. Immerse yourself in a celebration of creativity and tradition as you explore our rich collection, with a special focus on captivating indigenous fiber arts. Admire the intricate craftsmanship and vibrant hues of textiles that tell stories of ancestral wisdom and cultural heritage.
Image credit: Sarah Vombrack via Unsplash

Merriton Landing

Experience the vibrant pulse of modern entertainment at Merriton Landing, Taniti's rapidly evolving haven on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. This dynamic destination offers an array of exciting attractions, from a state-of-the-art movie theater that brings the latest blockbusters to life, to a pulsating dance club where the night comes alive with music and energy. And for those seeking a bit of friendly competition, the bowling alley adds a touch of excitement to the mix.
Image credit: Jakob Owens via Unsplash

Coming soon: Golfing

Get ready to tee off in paradise as Taniti unveils its upcoming golfing haven! Nestled amidst the island's breathtaking landscapes, our soon-to-be-developed golf course promises to be a golfer's dream, blending scenic beauty with an exciting challenge.
