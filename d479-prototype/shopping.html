On Taniti, every necessity is within reach.

Rest assured that your every need will be met with convenience on our tropical paradise. Taniti boasts two well-stocked supermarkets, providing a diverse array of local and international products for your shopping pleasure. For those seeking a more intimate shopping experience, two small grocery stores offer a curated selection of essentials, showcasing the unique flavors of the island. Additionally, a 24-hour convenience store stands ready to cater to your needs around the clock, ensuring that whether it's a midnight snack or last-minute travel essentials, Taniti has you covered.
