Stateside Grille

Taniti City

Experience the essence of American comfort food at Stateside Grille in Taniti City. Nestled in the heart of the metropolis, this restaurant offers classic American dishes with a modern flair, providing a cozy ambiance for patrons seeking familiar flavors.

Yellow Leaf Diner

Yellow Leaf Bay

Situated along the shores of Yellow Leaf Bay, Yellow Leaf Diner is a quintessential American-style eatery. From hearty breakfasts to savory burgers, this diner captures the essence of traditional American dining while offering a laid-back atmosphere by the bay.
Image credit: Dayanara Nacion via Unsplash

Merriton Smokehouse

Merriton Landing

Merriton Smokehouse in Merriton Landing is a barbecue haven where the smoky aroma of slow-cooked meats fills the air. This American-style smokehouse promises a flavorful journey through the heart of Taniti, combining the best of Southern barbecue with coastal vibes.
Image credit: Robert Bye via Unsplash

Zen Fusion

Taniti City

Taniti City welcomes Zen Fusion, an oasis of Pan-Asian flavors that transcends culinary boundaries. With a diverse menu inspired by the rich tapestry of Asian cuisines, this restaurant offers an exquisite dining experience in the heart of the city.

Sakura Grove

Yellow Leaf Bay

Nestled in the scenic surroundings of Yellow Leaf Bay, Sakura Grove is a Pan-Asian culinary escape. From sushi to stir-fries, this restaurant combines the freshest ingredients with breathtaking bay views, creating a harmonious fusion of flavors and scenery.
