Harbor Alehouse

Taniti City

Set against the backdrop of Taniti City's bustling streets, Harbor Alehouse is a lively pub that offers a cozy atmosphere to unwind with friends. With an extensive selection of craft beers and a menu featuring pub favorites, it's the perfect spot to enjoy a relaxed evening in the heart of the city.
Image credit: Qui Nguyen via Unsplash

Riverside Tavern

Taniti City

Riverside Tavern, nestled along the riverbanks in Taniti City, is a quintessential pub with a touch of local charm. The rustic ambiance and diverse drink menu make it a favorite among locals and visitors alike, providing a welcoming retreat from the urban hustle.
Image credit: Louis Hansel via Unsplash

Merriton Brew Haven

Merriton Landing

Merriton Landing welcomes beer enthusiasts to Merriton Brew Haven, a microbrewery that crafts small-batch brews with a local twist. Located in the heart of coastal entertainment, this microbrewery offers a unique blend of flavors, making it the perfect destination for those seeking a taste of Taniti's craft beer scene.
